About Ben Francis

Born in 1992 in the United Kingdom, Ben Francis discovered his passion for fitness early on. With an innate eye for design and entrepreneurial drive, he founded Gymshark at just 19—transforming a modest venture into a global fitness revolution.

His journey is marked by relentless determination, creative risk-taking, and a vision that transcends traditional fitness apparel—making Gymshark a symbol of innovation and community.

Personal Life & Background

Growing up in a modest yet supportive household in the United Kingdom, Ben learned the value of hard work and perseverance. His parents instilled in him resourcefulness, creativity, and the importance of family unity.

As the youngest of three, his early experiences in academics, sports, and community engagement laid the foundation for his innovative mindset and entrepreneurial spirit.
