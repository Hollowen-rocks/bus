About Ben Francis

Born on 4 June 1992 in the West Midlands, England, Ben Francis showed a natural aptitude for technology and innovation from a young age. His early ventures—ranging from selling car number plates online to self-teaching coding—foreshadowed the entrepreneurial journey ahead.

At 19, he co-founded Gymshark in his parents’ garage, merging his passion for fitness with his digital savvy. Today, Gymshark is a billion‑pound brand, celebrated for its disruptive marketing and strong community focus.

Personal Life

Growing up in Bromsgrove, Worcestershire, Ben’s upbringing was humble yet rich with values. His hardworking parents taught him the importance of perseverance, resourcefulness, and creative problem‑solving—lessons that would guide him throughout his life.

An avid fitness enthusiast from his teens, Ben’s love for the gym instilled in him a disciplined mindset. Today, he balances a global business empire with personal interests such as cars, motorcycles, and community service. Married to Robin Gallant and a dedicated father to twins (with another child on the way), his personal story is as inspiring as his professional success.

Deep Dive: In-depth Profile

Ben Francis was born on 4 June 1992 in the West Midlands and raised in Bromsgrove. At South Bromsgrove High School, his early fascination with IT and fitness led him to explore various online business ventures. These initial experiments—like selling car number plates—provided him with invaluable lessons in entrepreneurship.

His constant drive to learn and adapt laid the foundation for what would become Gymshark.

In 2012, Ben co-founded Gymshark with his school friend Lewis Morgan in his parents’ garage. Originally a supplements business, the company pivoted quickly to fitness apparel when Ben recognized a gap in the market for innovative gym wear.

A viral breakthrough in 2013, when a single tracksuit generated £30,000 in sales in 30 minutes, propelled Gymshark into the global spotlight. This rapid growth allowed Gymshark to secure major investments and achieve unicorn status by 2020.

At the core of Ben’s approach is the belief that "you get out what you put in." His commitment to discipline, innovation, and community has driven Gymshark’s success and inspired a new generation of entrepreneurs.

By fostering a culture of creativity and authenticity, Ben has transformed Gymshark into not just a brand, but a movement that champions fitness and self-improvement.
