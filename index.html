About Ben Francis

Born on 4 June 1992 in the West Midlands, England, Ben Francis showed a natural aptitude for technology and innovation from a young age. His early ventures—ranging from selling car number plates online to self-teaching coding—foreshadowed the entrepreneurial journey ahead.

At 19, he co-founded Gymshark in his parents’ garage, merging his passion for fitness with his digital savvy. Today, Gymshark is a billion‑pound brand, celebrated for its disruptive marketing and strong community focus.

Personal Life

Ben grew up in Bromsgrove, Worcestershire, where he was instilled with a strong work ethic by his parents. His passion for technology and fitness was apparent from a young age, spending his teenage years tinkering with computers and working at local gyms.

Outside of his business success, Ben is a family-oriented individual, enjoying time with his wife, Robin Gallant, and their young children. He is an avid car enthusiast and also has a love for motorcycles, frequently sharing his interests with his followers. Despite his rise to success, Ben remains grounded, with a commitment to supporting charitable causes and community initiatives.
